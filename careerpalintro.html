About Career Pal

Thank you for the opportunity and privilege to introduce the solutions and services offered by Career Pal Global.

Career pal Global is a social media platform that is created to offer solutions to students and institutions in making good use of technology so as to solve the following challenges:

Kenya / june 2021

I) Internship and job networking

• Currently, Kenya is ranked among the most affected countries as far as lack of jobs for graduates is concerned. Many professionals who graduate from different institutions end up in underpaid jobs or lack jobs completely. When students are not exposed to possible employers early enough while in college, this trend might continue for a long time. Career pal global seeks to break this trend through connecting students to relevant field of practice.

• Internships has been a challenge to many institutions and directly touches the office of career and industrial attachment. Through technology, organizations, companies, and government internship opportunities are linked to students automatically so as to avoid the hustle of seeking internship manually.


• International network. It’s a goal for almost every institution to produce individuals who are able cope with both local and international job market. Currently Kenya earns more money from Kenyans who work outside the country than what is earned through coffee tea, and major exports. Most of these opportunities were created through education exchange programs where students are connected to their assed fellows in other countries. Career pal takes the hustle to physically visit these institutions and takes the responsibility of the physical viability.

• Partial Scholarship-Through Career Pal Global sponsors, we will give partial scholarship to one bright and needy student.

Benefits of Career Pal Global to the Students

• Students will be facilitated to interact with assessed students from other institutions so as to encourage healthy socialization and to avoid fraud.

• Students will be able to have exchange programs both locally and internationally.

• Students will interact with potential employers for both internship and job opportunities.

We would like to build a unique relationship with you by offering quality and affordable services that will expand horizons for our young generation.

Our Program seeks to connect students in four continents, Europe, America, Asia and Africa.

How is it Implemented?

Upon subscription to the Career Pal Global App, each student will be networked with another student within East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda) for free. A student will be networked with 2 other friends from the remaining continents at a subscription fee of Ksh.50 for each country within Africa and Ksh.100 for networking with other students in Europe and Asian Countries.
